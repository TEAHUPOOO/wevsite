Buying Advice

Kitchen Buying Tips

💡 How We Test Kitchen Products

Every kitchen tool spends a minimum of 30 days in our test kitchen. We measure temperature accuracy, durability under repeated use, ease of cleaning, and real-world cooking results — not just spec sheets.

💰 When to Splurge vs. Save

Splurge on items you use daily (chef's knife, espresso machine). Save on items used weekly or less (specialty gadgets, single-use tools). A $300 knife used daily beats ten $30 knives replaced yearly.

🔍 What to Look For

Build materials matter most. Stainless steel over plastic, full-tang over partial. Check warranty length — brands confident in their products offer 5+ year coverage.
