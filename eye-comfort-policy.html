Eye Comfort Policy

At Best Home Gear Reviews, we know our readers spend considerable time on our deep-dive reviews and comparison guides. That's why we've implemented strict visual ergonomics across the site—our Eye Comfort Policy.

1. The Native Dark Mode Experience

Reading bright screens late at night is a leading cause of digital eye strain. To combat this, we've built a native Dark Mode toggle into our main navigation. Just click the 🌙 moon icon at the top right of your screen to instantly switch to a high-contrast, low-glare dark theme. Your preference is saved locally, so your eyes stay protected on every visit.

2. Typographic Readability

We use widely spaced, legible sans-serif fonts (Outfit for strong headings and Inter for precise body copy). Our body text features an optimized line height of 1.85, reducing the chance of readers "losing their place" and mitigating tracking fatigue during long review sessions.

3. Softer Color Palettes

You won't find harsh, pure whites (#FFFFFF) heavily dominating our reading backgrounds. Our default light theme utilizes a subtle, warm off-white (#FDFBF8) to reduce harsh blue-light glare and soften contrast ratios to comfortable, accessible standards.

4. Reduced Motion & Animations

While we use animations to make the site feel responsive, they are strictly limited to micro-interactions (like hover lifts). We avoid continuous looping animations and sudden layout shifts that can cause motion sickness or distract visual focus while reading.
